Case Study

At Phoenix Children's Hospitals, Cortex Neurometric carts have been in service since 2025. The PCH Neurodiagnostics Team reports that these carts have reduced workload in regards to cable management, organization, workflow during EEG studies, and cart maintenance while decreasing the risk of patients disrupting carts during sleep studies. In particular, the design decreases the footprint of previous carts while enclosing the full breadth of equipment within its protective chassis. This ease of use has improved technician morale and allowed for more valuable time to be spent on patients, rather than on maintenance of the cart itself. Technicians at Phoenix Children's Hospital, particularly those who had recently transitioned from other hospital networks, preferred our carts to the commonly available market competition, preferring the cleaner aesthetic, ease of movement, ease of infection control, streamlined cord management, and supply drawers.
